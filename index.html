Autumn's Embrace

Leaves of gold whisper and fall,
A chilly breeze whispers its call.
Gray clouds paint the sky's vast dome,
A gentle drizzle finds its home.

Swallows take flight, southward bound,
Seeking warmth on distant ground.
Golden fields of ripened grain,
Farmers rejoice, harvest's gain.

On a quiet path, serene and lone,
A child's light footsteps are known.
A smile as bright as morning's ray,
Warming hearts along the way.

Autumn brings a touch of sorrow's trace,
Yet hope's embrace fills every space.
Winter's chill will yield to spring's delight,
Life's vibrant hues will shine bright.
